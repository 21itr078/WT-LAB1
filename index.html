Kongu Engineering College (KEC)
KEC was founded by Kongu Vellalar Institute of Technology Trust (KVITT) in the year 1984. The college is affiliated to Anna University Chennai.

KEC is one of the multi-professional researched institutions.

The Institute provides an integral, inter-disciplinary education - a unique intersection between theory and practice, passion and reason. The college offers several undergraduate and postgraduate courses under technical, science, and management disciplines like B.Tech, B.Sc., M.Tech, MBA, and many more. Admissions to the courses are done on the basis of several entrance exams. The college is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) and accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with ‘A++’ Grade.
