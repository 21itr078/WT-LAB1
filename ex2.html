Welcome to Kongu National School

The Kongu National Matriculation Higher Secondary school, the fifth branch of KVIT Trust was established in the year 2002. Our school aims at an all round development of a child in academic, co-curricular and Extra-curricular activities. We assure our best in installing self-confidence, responsibility and concern towards society.

We achieve State Level rank in X, XI and XII public examinations, Sports and Games, Quiz competitions, Oratorical competitions and other co-scholastic areas.

We develop our children’s capabilities in Physical, Psychological and Intellectual development. Every student is taken care individually and personal attention is shown to upgrade his/her determination and discipline.
